Cooking Lasagna

Today we will look at how to cook one of my favourite dishes Lasagna!

This is a simple recipe that is quick and easy to cook.

Ingredients

  • Pasta Sheets
  • Courgette
  • Pasta Sauce
  • Chopped Tomatoes
  • Aubergine
  • Onion
  • Chickpeas
  • Carrots

Method

  1. First chop up all the vegetables
  2. Roast the veg for 30 mins
  3. Put a layer of the pasta sauce down into a casserole dish
  4. Then add chopped tomatoes and the vegetables
  5. Then add the pasta sheets
  6. Repeat the process creating 3 layers
  7. Add cheese on top
  8. Put in the oven for 30 minutes
